School Song's

O Childhood’s happy school
We sing Thy praise today
And hope that in our memories
Thy name will live always
That we as time goes on
May one day learn to be
True citizens and members brave
Of this our great country.
On on with God our guide
In all we say or do
A bulwork strong to help us on
Our life’s long journey through
that years to come may find us
Prepared to take our part
In life’s more serious lessons that
School days do only start.
We thank thee School of ours
For valiant hearts and true
For workers past and present and
Those of the future too.
Who have striven and still strive
To guide our steps aright
Along life’s dim uncertain paths
To heaven’s Eternal Light
