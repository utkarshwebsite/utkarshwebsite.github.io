School Logo

The Light Of Christ Shines To All

The School emphasizes on the development of leadership qualities, service to the society and enhancement of knowledge and wisdom. The purpose of imparting education in this institution is clearly visible in it’s logo.


The logo of the School comprises a shield carrying a mitre in the top left section, a cross on the right, a torch in the bottom left and a crosier and a book in the right. Above the shield, a ribbon carries the School name, and another ribbon with the School motto keeps the shield together. Colours used in the logo are blue and gold.

Blue and Gold symbolizes widom

Mitre - The sign of Leadership

Mitre is a type of headgear now known as the traditional, ceremonial head-dress of bishops and certain abbots in traditional Christianity.

Cross - The sign of Sacrifice

“God so loved the world that He gave His only begotton Son, so that whoever believeth in Him shall not die but have everlasting life.” Jesus was crucifield on the cross for the sins of the world.



Torch - The sign of Hope

Torch held up symbolizes life, truth and the regenerative power o f flame. The torch is an emblem of enlightenment.



Crosier & a Book - Symbolizes Authority & Knowledge

A crosier is a staff carried by high-ranking Catholic, Anglican, and other denomination prelates, as a symbol of authority. Book is the source of knowledge.



Shield & the Ribbons - The sign Authority & Knowledge

The Shield represents security, power and control. It upholds the School name above everything and is held together by the School motto: “The Light of Christ Shines To All”. The shield gives the ultimate protection against the uncertainties of the future, and helps one in becoming an affective leader, who sacrifices himself to serve the world, bears the torch of enlightenment, and spreads knowledge with authority.
