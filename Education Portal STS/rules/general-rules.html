General Rules

  1. At the beginning of each session if the student is absent for the first three days without leave or prior information his/her name may be struck off from the School Scholar Register. Re-admission may be considered only if vacancy exists.
  2. All students must report to school at the reporting time.
  3. The school authorities will not be responsible for the children after the school hours outside the School campus.
  4. The School does not provide any transport facility for commuting to and from the school. Parents must make sure that the mode of transport selected by them for their ward, does not carry children more than its capacity, is safe and follows the traffic rules. The School will not be responsible for any mishap outside the School premises.
  5. Parents/guardians of children are requested to sign –
    a) The notices sent out periodically.
    b) The assignments given to the children for home work.
    c) Exercise books in which work has been assessed.
  6. In all correspondence with the school, name, class, and section of the child must be stated clearly. Parents/guardians must write their names and contact numbers below their signature.
  7. Tuitions are not promoted by the School, however for those parents who want their wards to take extra classes from the teachers of the school, will only be allowed after the written permission has been obtained from the Principal. Such permission will only be given for the week students (scoring less than 60% marks in a particular subject). All tuitions must stop at least 7 days before the examination in all the classes. No teacher will take any new tuition after the second term tests (from December onwards).
  8. All parents/guardians must ensure that their children have prepared their lessons well and have completed their homework, if any, before coming to school.
  9. Parents/guardians must check the school dairy for any reports made by the teachers. The reports if any must be signed by them.
  10. Students must not damage the school property. They must not write on the walls or on the furniture else they will be fined for it. Throwing of ink or paint is a punishable offence. Whiteners and staplers are banned in school. Festival Colours, fire crackers, mobile phones, digital gadgets and anything that can be injurious are strictly prohibited and carrying such things on the school campus is a punishable offence.
  11. All parents/guardians must take the permission slip from the office before meeting any of the staff members in School, during the school hours.
  12. Once a child enters the School campus will not be sent back home for any reason before the school gives over.
  13. Parents must make sure that their ward(s) are punctual to school. Two late comings in a month are exempted, third time the ward(s) will be marked absent and will be sent back home.
  14. Parents may come to meet the teachers to discuss their ward(s) behaviour/performance for one hour after school on any working Saturday. Parents must make sure that they meet the teachers at least once in a month.
  15. NO RE-TEST/RE-EXAM WILL BE CONDUCTED DUE TO ANY REASON.
  16. Minimum 80% attendance is compulsory to appear in the examination/term test.
  17. The child will be marked zero is he or she is absent in any test or exam. Submission of a medical certificate in the final examination does not guarantee promotion to the next class.

School Fee Regulations

  1. The fee must be deposited in the bank/school fee office on time. The details of dates of submission of fees are mentioned on the fee book .Cash will be deposited in the bank and only cheques will be deposited in the school office.
  2. The late installments with the fine will be deposited in the school fee office in cash only.
  3. The time to submit the school fee in the School office is 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. on all working days.
  4. A late fee of Rs. 5/- will be charged every day after the last due date (as given in the fee book) of every unpaid fee installment.
  5. The School will not be responsible for any error committed by the parent/guardian while depositing the fee through cheque.
  6. Bounced cheques will be considered a default, for which the parent/guardian will have to bear the bank charges/late fees/both “if any” in any such case.
  7. Outstation cheques will not be accepted.
  8. For every NEFT/RTGS, parent/guardian must give the Transaction ID and get the fee book stamped by the School Office for the installment paid, to avoid inconvenience.
  9. Fee defaulters will not be permitted to appear for the examination/test until all dues are paid at least 5 days before the examination/test.
  10. The fee slips (teacher’s copy) must be sent to class teacher on time for the maintenance of record.
  11. Any fees paid is non-refundable and non-adjustable in any condition.
  12. Term fees includes electricity and water charges, healthcare, games, diary, furniture, books in the library, home examinations etc.
  13. Charges:
    a) Transfer Certificate 150/-
    b) Reissue of School Diary 150/-
    c) Duplicate T.C. 500/-
    d) Duplicate Report Card 500/-

WITHDRAWALS

One month’s notice must be given through an application from the day of submitting the duly filled prescribed T.C. form available in the office before a student is withdrawn from the School. One month’s full fee must be paid in lieu of one month’s notice in case of sudden requirement of the T.C.. Students absent for a long period and applying for T.C. will be charged full fee up till the month in which the T.C. has been applied for.
